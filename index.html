About the Engine

Uyo Logistics Solutions provides an autonomous, high-precision routing engine built strictly for delivery fleets operating within Uyo. Generic map apps fail to account for hyper-local geometric realities, resulting in inaccurate ETAs, wasted fuel, and unnecessary carbon emissions.
